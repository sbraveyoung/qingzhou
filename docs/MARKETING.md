## 2. 应用商店描述(精炼宣发版)

**中文(~80 字):**
> 轻舟是一款原生、轻量、零数据收集的 iOS / macOS 网络配置工具。多协议节点订阅、智能分流、实时流量与域名分析,一切只存在你的设备上。开源可审计,不运营节点 —— 你自备节点,它负责优雅转发。

**English (~60 words):**
> Qingzhou is a native, lightweight, zero-data-collection network utility for iOS & macOS.
> Manage multi-protocol node subscriptions, route traffic by your own rules, and inspect live
> traffic and per-domain analytics — all stored only on your device. Open-source and auditable.
> Bring your own nodes; it just forwards, elegantly.

## 3. 社群宣发短文(面向海外华人社区)

**中文(~200 字):**
> **轻舟 / Qingzhou — 一款给"对隐私和设计都讲究的人"的网络配置工具**
>
> 我们做了一款原生 iOS / macOS 工具:它不收集你任何数据,不接任何第三方 SDK,节点 / 订阅 / 规则全部只存在你自己的设备上。它**不提供节点** —— 节点你自己有,轻舟负责把流量按你的规则优雅转发。
>
> 支持 trojan / vmess / vless / shadowsocks / hysteria2 和 Clash 导入;有实时流量波形、域名分析(哪些域名走了代理 / 直连、命中哪条规则)、自动测速择优、切换自动重启、快捷指令一键开关这些顺手的细节。
>
> App 代码 MIT 开源,你能看清它到底做了什么。名字取自"轻舟已过万重山" —— 轻盈地越过阻碍,而不必把阻碍画在脸上。
>
> 即将上架国际区 App Store。源码与进展:github.com/sbraveyoung/qingzhou

**English (~120 words):**
> **Qingzhou — a network utility for people who care about privacy *and* craft.**
>
> A native iOS / macOS tool that collects **zero** data, embeds **no** third-party SDKs, and keeps
> your nodes, subscriptions, and rules **only on your device**. It doesn't sell or run any nodes —
> you bring your own; it just forwards traffic by your rules, elegantly.
>
> Supports trojan / vmess / vless / shadowsocks / hysteria2 and Clash import, with live traffic
> waveforms, per-domain analytics, auto latency-testing, hot-switch on node/mode change, and
> Shortcuts/Siri toggles. MIT-licensed and auditable.
>
> Coming soon to the international App Store. Source: github.com/sbraveyoung/qingzhou
